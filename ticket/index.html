Ticket FAQ

Will there be walk-in tickets available?

No, unfortunately we cannot offer walk-in tickets on ACA Day due to a limited capacity of only 40 people. The last day to buy a ticket is May 16th.

Can I buy more than one ticket at a time?

No, please follow the steps above for each person buying a ticket. If you must e-transfer from the same address, please send one transfer for each ticket.

Can I pay with cash?

Yes, you can pay with cash by coming in-person to our ACA meeting on Thursday nights at 7pm. We are located at Faith Lutheran Church in Kitchener (247 Westmount Road E).

What is included in the ticket?

Your $45 ticket includes breakfast, lunch, snacks, drinks (juice, pop, coffee) and all required supplies for our art workshop.

Is a 7th tradition contribution required?

No, but if you have the financial ability to, we are asking for your help. We are trying to raise money to buy literature inventory and run future events, as lain out on our budget.
